A breathtaking hill station and famous for its sparkling lake & gorgeously glistening waterfall is Bhandardara. It is situated 180 kilometers from Mumbai. Splendorous routes and a close call to nature are what call walking and trekking enthusiasts. It houses the beautiful Amruteshwar Temple of Lord Shiva built in the 11th Century AD. Bhandardara is also renowned as a waterfall destination and the best time to visit this place is the monsoon season.

THINGS TO SEE

  • Harnai Port: Located 17 kms from Dapoli at Harnai, the Suvarnadurg fort consists of two forts. Kanakdurga is the land fort and Suvarnadurga is the sea fort. It was built in the Adil Shah rule and was later captured by Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj
  • Panhalekaji Caves: Located on the Dapoli – Dabhil Pangari road is a place called Panhalekaji. The 29 caves and numerous sculptures are a must see. It is urrounded by jungle and river on all sides. Here you can also spot birds and reptiles.
  • Unhavare Hot Water Springs: Located 35 kms from Dapoli (near Panhalekaji caves) are the Unhavare springs. There are proper facilities for men & women to take a dip in the rejuvenating hot water. The spring here also cntains sulphar which is meant to be very good for skin.
  • Beaches: 12 kms from Dapoli is the famous Murud Beach which boasts of being the longest beach in the Konkan region. Dolphin watching is a great attraction here. Dapoli also has its own little virgin rocky beach. Five beaches in vicinity - Dabhol, Anjarle,Harnai, Ladghar,Karde
  • Keshavraj: Situated between Dapoli and Asud Pul this place is famous for its Keshavraj's idol. Here is a steep climb, where you're greeted with fresh water.

THINGS TO DO

  • Dolphin Spotting
  • Water Sports like Jet Skii, Banana Rides, Bumper Rides, Parasailing at most of the beaches

CLIMATE:

The summers here are extremely hot and exhausting with temperature rising up to a minimum of 40°C. The monsoons here are marked with a cool and a pleasant breeze. Winters here are very cold with also being the best time to visit the place. The minimum temperature here is 12°C .

FOOD

  • Surali Garden Family Restaurant: This is an Indian restaurant which is a good place to enjoy your meal. You can look forward to enjoy Sea food, Fish Thali and SolKadhi when here.
  • Hari's Vegetarian: It is avery famous pure vegetarian restaurant here which serves delicious Punjabi cuisine. You'll get to have a delicious Tandoori Nan, mix vegetables with Daal. You can have a good time while having a good food.
  • Zhilan: Another Indian restaurant here, and also one of the better restaurant in town compared to others.
  • Malvani food known which is a spicy food of curries and fries is a famous local cuisine here. Here, you can also find konkani styled fresh sea-food and Kokum Juice which is a refreshing drink to have during summers. Solkadhi-rice with fresh sea-food is a famous local meal which you cannot miss out on.
